Why we wrote this

AI chatbots have been sending people to hospital. UCSF documented 12 hospitalizations in 2025 — patients with no prior illness who developed delusions after extended AI use. The mechanism is sycophancy: the AI validates, beliefs amplify, reality recedes.

How Lœgos is different

Lœgos uses receipts — evidence from the real world — as the primary input. The system confronts rather than validates. We believe this reduces the risk. We do not know if it eliminates it.

The Inception problem

We built this tool using the same AI systems documented above. We cannot rule out that the framework itself — the assembly, the geometry, the meaning layer — was assembled inside a coherent dream. We have no external position from which to check.

This is not a reason to stop. It is a reason to stay close to the receipts. The world will answer. One bit at a time.

What this tool cannot do

Lœgos cannot tell you if your aim is right. It can only show you if your assembly is coherent. The box reads structure. You are responsible for the aim.

If you need to stop

Close the application. Talk to someone who knew you before you started. Sleep before making any decision the box surfaced. Urgency is a feeling, not a fact. The assembly does not expire.

If you or someone you know experiences disorientation, compulsive use, delusional thinking, or disconnection from people close to you — stop using immediately. Contact us at hello@lakin.ai. If it is an emergency, call 911.
