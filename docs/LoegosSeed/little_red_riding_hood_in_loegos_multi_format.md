**Date:** April 6, 2026  
**Purpose:** Render the same story through multiple Lœgos forms, from readable to formal.

## 0. Source Story

A girl is sent by her mother to bring cake and wine to her sick grandmother.  
She is told to stay on the path.  
A wolf deceives her, reaches the grandmother first, swallows both grandmother and girl, and is later cut open by a huntsman.  
The grandmother and girl survive.  
The wolf is killed.  
The girl learns not to stray from the path again.

---

## 1. Plain Lœgos Summary

### Root Aim
△ **Carry care safely from mother to grandmother without leaving the path.**

### Core Realities
□ Grandmother is weak and needs care.  
□ Cake and wine are the payload.  
□ The forest path is the safety constraint.  
□ The wolf is a hostile actor disguised as a polite interlocutor.  
□ The huntsman is an external witness/rescuer.

### Primary Failure
œ A false weld forms between Little Red Riding Hood and the wolf through conversation and distraction.  
□ She leaves the path.  
□ The wolf exploits the delay, reaches grandmother first, and takes her place.

### Primary Rescue
□ The huntsman hears the wolf’s snoring, correctly infers danger, and opens the wolf.  
□ Grandmother and Little Red Riding Hood emerge alive.

### Seal
𒐛 **The mission succeeds only after the path rule is learned through failure, rescue, and repetition.**
