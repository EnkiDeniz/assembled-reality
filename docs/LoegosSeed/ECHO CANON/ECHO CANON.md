**I. PRESENCE**  
  
Before aim.  
  
Truth is self-evident.  
  
Sense precedes speech.  
  
Be first; then move.  
  
Move follows presence.  
  
Stillness selects the step.  
  
Presence makes time enough.  
  
The pulse is the plan.

△

A triangle ends wandering.

Point smaller; arrive sooner.

Aim chooses; options release.  
  
Name arrival in seven words.

**III. EVIDENCE**  
  
□  
  
Reality speaks in receipts.  
  
Touch the world, not the idea.  
  
Friction is testimony.  
  
Squares don’t argue; they measure.  
  
What changed? Say one fact.  
  
Physics is a kind editor.  
  
Story follows proof, not hope.  
  
Meaning lands where evidence sits.  
  
Dots, then lines.

**IV. FLOW**  
  
○

The logbook remembers without blame.

Close the loop; free the mind.

Failure is a map.  
  
Coherence is a paid invoice.  
  
Time rewards the trace.

**V. ACTION**  
  
One obvious move or none.  
  
Five minutes or it’s fantasy.  
  
Shrink the move until kind.  
  
Make it measurable, then move.  
  
Tiny tests tame giant claims.  
  
If it doesn’t change reality, it’s rehearsal.  
  
Practice proves.  
  
Play is disciplined curiosity.  
  
⸻

7

Seal is where claim meets evidence and a validator.  
  
Aim becomes buildable at the seal.  
  
Tell it after the seal.  
  
A receipt is a soft gavel.  
  
Proof ends the meeting.  
  
Seven is the quiet yes.  
  
Evidence makes authority portable.  
  
The seal summons the test.

**VII. GRACE**  
  
∞  
  
No shame, only signal.

Grace lets the atom reform.

Reversible is responsible.  
  
Mercy keeps momentum humane.  
  
Consent before compute.

Tag the mix before you breathe it.
